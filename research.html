Neuron Local Transcriptome and Translatome Meta-Analysis

Supervised by UK DRI Emerging Leader
Dr. Julie Qiaojin Lin, I integrated transcriptome and translatome sequencing data from different compartments of the neuron across different species.

We hoped to identify the common differential expressed genes and abundant genes, providing clues for further studies on neuronal functions and disorders

The paper manuscript is now being prepared.
